# Job Finder™ — AI-Powered Career Search Engine

## Overview

Job Finder™ is a new feature inside [Career-Ops](https://careerops.org?utm_source=chatgpt.com) that automatically searches the internet for jobs based on a user’s Career Matching™ profile, resume, skills, and target career paths.

Instead of manually searching through thousands of listings, Job Finder™ acts like an AI recruiter that:

* searches the web for relevant jobs
* ranks them by compatibility
* scores each opportunity
* explains why the role matches
* and prioritizes the highest-probability opportunities first

---

# Core Concept

Career Matching™ identifies:

> “What careers are you best suited for?”

Job Finder™ answers:

> “What jobs available right now are the best fit for you?”

The two systems work together seamlessly.

# AI Match Scoring System

Every job receives a personalized:

# Match Score™

Example:

| Job Title                           | Match Score |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------- |
| Product Manager — Spotify           | 96%         |
| EdTech Program Manager — Google     | 93%         |
| AI Solutions Consultant — Microsoft | 89%         |

The score is based on:

* skills overlap
* industry experience
* seniority alignment
* education
* transferable experience
* keyword compatibility
* leadership signals
* portfolio/projects
* ATS compatibility

---

# Ranked Search Results

Results are automatically sorted:

## Highest Match Score → Lowest Match Score

The best opportunities appear first.

This creates:

* less application waste
* higher interview probability
* more strategic job searching
