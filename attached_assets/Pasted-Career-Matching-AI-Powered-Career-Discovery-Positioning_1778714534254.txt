Career Matching™ — AI-Powered Career Discovery & Positioning Engine
Overview
Career Matching™ is a new AI feature inside Career-Ops that analyzes a user’s resume, experience, education, projects, and skills to identify careers and job titles they are most naturally aligned with.
Instead of only matching users to a single job description, Career Matching™ helps users discover:
what careers they are already qualified for
hidden strengths in their background
transferable skills
industries where they can compete
and how to position themselves professionally online
It functions like an AI career strategist, recruiter, and branding consultant combined.
